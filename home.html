About Me

My name is Jillian Desmond, I'm a junior Computer Science major and Computer Networking minor.
I'm interested in Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity. On campus, I work as an Admissions Ambassador.
As a woman in a male dominated field and University, I'm passionate about getting more women interested in STEM

fascinated by making my own. Luckily, Wentworth offers a course "AI for Gaming". I'm excited to utilize my
co op semesters to experience different applications of my major and narrow my focus.
